Navigation pane

Navigation pane is the most important control element in this application. By selecting one of included navigation panel you affect other displayed elements of the user interface, such as active page, object inspector, the ribbon bar, etc.

You can easily customize the navigation pane layout and its visual appearance to your needs, by using its drop down menu.
The navigation pane may be located to the left or to the right side of the main window, you can collapse it to a minimum width, buttons (panels) can be displayd at bottom by default, but also on left or on right.

Warning!
Individual buttons can be removed from the panel by the "Add or remove buttons" function, but keep in mind that this will also disable the navigation for given part of an application (including the main menu and ribbon bar options).
